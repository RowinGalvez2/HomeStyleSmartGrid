Smart Grid Solution for In-home Energy Management

Problem

As the demand for energy continues to grow due to increased electronic usage, our reliance on traditional energy grids not only strains the system but also drives up electric bills.

Problem Statement

The goal is to develop and deploy a feasible smart grid system tailored for personal in-home use, effectively leveraging advanced technologies to intelligently manage electricity distribution from various sources, thus improving energy efficiency and adaptability to changing consumer demands. This project aims to streamline energy flow through the implementation of smart grid technology, addressing the distinctive energy consumption patterns and intricacies of in-home environments, with a primary focus on feasibility.
